# Fashion Research & Content Database

## Clothing Categories

### Dresses
- **Bodycon Dress**: Tight-fitting dress that hugs the body, often made of stretchy material
- **Maxi Dress**: Long, flowing dress that reaches the ankles, perfect for formal events
- **Fit-and-Flare Dress**: Fitted at the waist and flares out towards the hem
- **Shift Dress**: Loose-fitting, straight-cut dress that doesn't hug the body
- **Sheer Dress**: Transparent fabrics making bold statements
- **White Dresses**: Statement snowy pieces in chiffon, silk, taffeta
- **Knit Dresses**: Wool or knitted material for warmth and comfort
- **Sweater Dresses**: Thicker, cozy option for winter season

### Tops
- **Blouses**: Structured design with ruffles, pleats, or embroidery
- **Peplum Blouses**: Flared ruffle at the waist creating hourglass silhouette
- **Bohemian Blouses**: Loose-fitting with ethnic prints or embellishments
- **Pussy Bow Blouses**: Bow tie at collar for elegant professional look
- **Sheer Tops**: Diaphanous chiffon to delicate lace
- **Crochet Tops**: Bohemian charm with textured details
- **Polo Shirts**: Country club mood making comeback

### Bottoms
- **Barrel Jeans**: Champion silhouette with varying fullness
- **Wide-Leg Trousers**: Clean lines and simple silhouettes
- **Pencil Skirts**: Tailored and demure styling
- **Sheer Skirts**: Transparent fabrics for bold statements
- **Maxi Skirts**: Flowing and comfortable
- **Shorts**: Micro shorts trending from runway
- **Leggings**: Form-fitting for workouts or casual wear

### Outerwear
- **Barn Jackets**: Elevated workwear aesthetic
- **Trench Coats**: Classic with modern updates
- **Bomber Jackets**: Leather and suede variations
- **Faux Fur Coats**: Luxurious texture for winter
- **Quilted Jackets**: Practical yet stylish

## Styling Tips

### Color Coordination
- Monochromatic looks in neutral tones
- Burgundy paired with cream or camel
- Pastels mixed with white for freshness
- Metallics as statement accent pieces

### Proportion Play
- Oversized bags with fitted clothing
- Mini bags with flowing silhouettes
- Cropped jackets with high-waisted bottoms
- Layered lengths for visual interest

### Occasion Dressing
- Day to night transitions with accessories
- Office appropriate with structured pieces
- Evening elegance with luxe materials
- Weekend casual with relaxed fits
